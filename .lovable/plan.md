

# Stanley Agyei – Personal Portfolio Website

## Overview
A modern, single-page portfolio website for Stanley Agyei — a Data & Systems Analyst, Web Developer, and GIS/Land Management Specialist. The site will be visually striking with deep blues, earthy greens, and smooth animations, reflecting Stanley's unique blend of tech and geospatial expertise.

---

## Design & Style
- **Color palette**: Deep navy (#1e3a5f, #0a1929), earthy greens (#2e7d32, #4caf50), light neutrals (#f5f5f5)
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif headings (Inter/Poppins), readable body text
- **Layout**: Full-width sections, asymmetric grids, generous whitespace, subtle shadows
- **Animations**: Smooth scroll, animated counters, hover effects on cards, fade-in on scroll
